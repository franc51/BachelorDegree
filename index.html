Welcome to the presentation website of my bachelor project.

My name is Francisc Szasz, and I am student at Transilvania University, more precisely at Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computers. My aim for the bachelor project is to achieve a product that has the same quality and properties as a commercial one. Besides, for learning purposes, the final result has to be compared with simulations, to understand the challenges that come when designing an audio amplifier from scratch.

The presentation does not include development and any sort of calculations, only a brief resume of the main components and their role. For more information you can contact me.
