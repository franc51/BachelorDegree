What is the role of an audio amplifier? 

        In a HI-FI acoustic system, the audio power amplifier has a special importance: on it depends the fidelity of the
        signal reproduction and, at the same time, the power that it can deliver to the load with a minimum distortion 
        factor.
        
        The role of an audio power amplifier is to charge the required power in the load, under the conditions of a high 
        energy efficiency, a lower dissipated power of the final transistors, a higher power amplification and a lower 
        distortion factor.
